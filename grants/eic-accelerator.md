# EIC Accelerator (European Innovation Council)

## Overview

The EIC Accelerator is the EU's flagship program for supporting high-risk, high-impact deep-tech startups and SMEs. It provides grant funding up to EUR 2.5 million and equity investments from the EIC Fund between EUR 1 million and EUR 10 million.

The 2026 Work Programme introduced a major simplification with 6 batching dates per year (up from 2 in 2025) and a streamlined evaluation process.

**Total 2026 budget:** EUR 630 million
**Website:** https://eic.ec.europa.eu/eic-funding-opportunities/eic-accelerator_en

## Eligibility

- **Single SME** applicant (no consortium required)
- Registered in an EU member state or Horizon Europe associated country (Norway qualifies via EEA)
- Must be a for-profit entity
- Technology Readiness Level: **TRL 5-8** (validated in relevant environment to system complete and qualified)
- Must demonstrate potential to create new markets or disrupt existing ones
- Cannot have received EIC Accelerator grant funding previously under Horizon Europe (grant-only once)
- Revenue or investment should demonstrate market traction

### 2026 Strategic Challenges (5 priority areas)
1. Advanced Materials for Renewable Energy and Energy Storage Systems
2. Alternative Concepts and Key Enabling Technologies for Fusion Power Plants
3. Deep Tech for Climate Adaptation
4. Resilient and Trusted Connectivity
5. Biotechnologies for Food System Sustainability

**Zipminator fits:** #4 (Resilient and Trusted Connectivity) and Open category (any deep tech)

## Application Requirements

### Stage 1: Short Proposal (Rolling)
- Written proposal (short form)
- Pitch deck (PDF)
- 3-minute video pitch
- Processing time: 4-6 weeks
- Result: GO / NO GO

### Stage 2: Full Proposal (Batching Dates)
Invited applicants with GO from Stage 1:

1. **Part A:** Administrative forms (online)
2. **Part B:** Technical proposal (max 20 pages):
   - Section 1: Excellence
   - Section 2: Impact
   - Section 3: Implementation
3. **Pitch deck** (PDF, updated)
4. **3-minute video pitch** (updated)
5. **Implementation plan:** Gantt chart, milestones, deliverables
6. **Financial information:** Business plan, financial projections, cap table
7. **Letters of Intent** from customers, partners, investors
8. **Freedom to Operate (FTO) analysis**

### Stage 3: Interview
- Remote interview with expert panel (3+ evaluators)
- Approximately 3-4 weeks after batching date
- 10-minute presentation + Q&A
- Technology expert assessment in parallel

### Evaluation Criteria
- **Excellence:** Breakthrough innovation, technology risk, IP position
- **Impact:** Market potential, scalability, team capability
- **Implementation:** Work plan quality, financial plan, milestones
- Each criterion scored on GO/NO GO basis
