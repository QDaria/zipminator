# Digital Europe Programme (DEP) -- Cybersecurity and Trust

## Overview

The Digital Europe Programme (DEP) supports deployment of digital capacities across the EU, including cybersecurity. The European Cybersecurity Competence Centre (ECCC) manages Specific Objective 3: Cybersecurity and Trust under the DEP Regulation (EU) 2021/694.

The Digital Europe Cybersecurity Work Programme 2025-2027 allocates up to EUR 50 million across multiple calls, with focus areas directly relevant to PQC transition.

In April 2025, the EU adopted a Coordinated Implementation Roadmap for the Transition to Post-Quantum Cryptography, mandating all member states begin PQC transition by end of 2026, with critical infrastructure protected by end of 2030. Norway, as an EEA/EFTA country, participates in Digital Europe.

**Website:** https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/activities/digital-programme
**ECCC:** https://cybersecurity-centre.europa.eu/

## Eligibility

- Legal entities established in EU member states or associated countries (Norway is associated via EEA)
- SMEs, large enterprises, public bodies, and research organizations eligible
- Specific calls may have consortium requirements (varies by call)
- Some deployment calls accept single applicants; others require multi-country consortia
- Must align with the specific call's objectives and scope

## Draft Application

### Target: PQC Deployment for Critical Infrastructure

#### 1. Project Title

**PQ-DEPLOY: Deploying Post-Quantum Cryptography for European Critical Infrastructure Operators**

#### 2. Call Reference

DIGITAL-ECCC-2026-DEPLOY-CYBER-[NUMBER] (or subsequent PQC-specific deployment call)

#### 3. Project Summary

PQ-DEPLOY will accelerate the EU's post-quantum cryptography transition by deploying QDaria's Zipminator platform -- a production-grade PQC suite with Kyber768 KEM -- across critical infrastructure operators in 3+ EU/EEA countries. The project directly implements the EU's Coordinated Implementation Roadmap for PQC Transition by providing turnkey PQC migration tools for energy, telecom, and public sector organizations.

#### 4. Alignment with EU PQC Roadmap

The EU PQC Roadmap (April 2025) requires:
- All member states to have a national PQC strategy by end of 2026
- Critical infrastructure to transition to PQC by end of 2030
- Pilots and first steps to begin immediately

PQ-DEPLOY addresses these requirements by:
- Providing a deployable PQC platform (not just research prototypes)
- Demonstrating PQC transition in 3 critical infrastructure sectors
- Producing reusable migration guides and toolkits for other operators
- Training infrastructure operators in PQC deployment
