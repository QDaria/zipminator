## Notes

- Norway participates in Horizon Europe and Digital Europe as an EEA/EFTA associated country.
- US programs (DARPA, NSF) require a US entity or partner; consider establishing a US subsidiary or partnering with a US institution.
- NATO DIANA is open to companies from NATO member states; Norway is a founding NATO member.
- Several programs can be combined (stacked) as long as the same costs are not double-funded.
- The Norwegian Quantum Initiative is the single largest opportunity given Zipminator's direct relevance to quantum-safe cryptography.
