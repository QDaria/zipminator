## 📝 POSTING TIPS

### Best Times to Post on LinkedIn:
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 10:00-11:00 AM or 1:00-2:00 PM (local time)
- **Avoid**: Weekends and Mondays before 8 AM

### Engagement Strategies:
1. **Tag Collaborators**: @Rigetti Computing, @QDaria (if they have LinkedIn pages)
2. **Comment on Your Own Post**: Add a first comment with additional context or question
3. **Respond Quickly**: Reply to all comments within first 2 hours for maximum reach
4. **Reshare After 7 Days**: LinkedIn's algorithm allows effective resharing after a week

### Hashtag Strategy:
- **Use 5-10 hashtags** (more than 10 reduces reach)
- **Mix popular and niche**: #QuantumComputing (850K followers) + #PostQuantumCryptography (niche)
- **Include location**: #Norway, #Oslo, #NordicStartups

### Video Link Strategy:
- **Don't post just the link**: LinkedIn penalizes external links
- **Add context BEFORE the link**: Write compelling copy first, then add URL
- **Consider native video**: If you have the video file, upload directly to LinkedIn for 5x better reach (but YouTube link works for broader distribution)
